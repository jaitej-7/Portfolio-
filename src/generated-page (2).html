Methodology

The Creative Cycle

01

Empathize

Every great product starts with deep understanding. I immerse myself in the user's world through interviews and data to uncover latent needs and pain points.

02

Define

Clarifying the chaos. I synthesize research into actionable insights and a clear problem statement that guides the entire design strategy.

03

Ideate

No idea is too wild. Through sketching and wireframing, I explore divergent paths to find the most innovative and feasible solutions.

04

Prototype

Bringing the vision to life. I craft high-fidelity designs and interactive mockups that look and feel like the final product.

05

Test

Validation is key. I put prototypes in front of users to gather feedback, ensuring the solution actually solves the problem effectively.
